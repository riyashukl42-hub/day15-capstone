# Day 15 — Capstone: End-to-End Consumer Health Analytics

## The brief
A consumer health company needs to understand which customers are most
valuable, most at health risk, and what the financial case is for
a targeted wellness programme.

## Skills applied
Every skill from the 15-day challenge in one project:
EDA · RFM Segmentation · Data Cleaning · Risk Stratification
Random Forest ML · Feature Importance · Business Recommendation

## Datasets
- Supermarket Sales — 1,000 transactions, 3 branches
- Medical Insurance Cost — 1,338 patients (1,199 after outlier removal)

## Key results
- Champions avg lifetime spend: £542.83
- At Risk customers: 40 — immediate win-back campaign needed
- High Risk patients: 65 (5.4% of population)
- ROC-AUC: 0.96 — excellent model discrimination
- Smoker cost premium: £13,659/year
- 50 successful smoking cessation interventions = £682,950 annual saving

## Strategic recommendation
Target 40-60 Champions who are also High Risk for a premium wellness
programme. Dual ROI: protect highest retail spend AND reduce highest
insurance cost burden simultaneously.

## Priority intervention
Smoking cessation programme for high-value customers:
£13,659 annual saving per quitter — permanently compounding.
